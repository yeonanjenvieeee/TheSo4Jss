ABOUT US

Welcome to So4Js! We are an innovative platform dedicated to expanding knowledge and skills in the field of technology. The So4Js is a diverse team of passionate researchers and a group from Grade 11 ICT students of Tapinac Senior High School, aims to share knowledge and inspiration with fellow ICT students. The goal of So4Js project is to provide a services and resources that will help individuals to enhance their programming skills and also to find encouragement, motivation, fun and excitement in programming field.
